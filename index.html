GARLIC BREAD

Recipe by: jessyratfink

I found the recipe was from a website named Instructables. I chose a garlic bread recipe since garlic bread is my favorite food, and this was the easiest recipe I can find. Garlic Bread is the best kind of bread that there is and it is a very easy food to make.

Steps on how to make Garlic Bread:

  1. Prepare all the ingredients (one fresh baguette that is sliced in half lengthwise and then into several pieces, 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, 1/2 teaspoon salt, two tablespoons chopped parsley, three cloves garlic that's minced, and 1/8 - 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese), a baking sheet, and set the oven to 425 Fahrenheit.
  2. Melt + Spread. Melt the butter in a small bowl and stir in the rest of the ingredients. Use the back of a spoon (works the best and doesn't ruin the bread yay!) to smooth the mixture over the bread.
  3. Bake! Bake the garlic bread at 425 F for 10-15 minutes. Keep an eye on it and pull it out as soon as it starts to brown around the edges, otherwise it'll dry out too much. Enjoy!
